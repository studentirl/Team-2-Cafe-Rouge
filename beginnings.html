Our Beginnings

The story behind Café Rouge.

1984

Where It All Started

Karen Jones and Roger Myers met as Hospitality students The two often discussed running a restaurant together, and being flatmates gave them years ample time to experiment with different dishes together. Their shared loved of French cuisine formed a tradition of post-exams bistro dining. With hard work, strong time management and valuable help from the Student Enterprise Centre, Karen and Roger went on to graduate with First Class degrees and a solid business plan.

1987

Dream to Reality

The summer post-graduation was spent on a culinary tour of France. The experience sent Karen and Roger's mission to recreate authentic French food and wine in a relaxed bistro-style setting.

1989

Launch

Our first restaurant opened in Richmond, London.

1996

Café Rouge Mentioned in a Bestseller

In 1996, Café Rouge became a regular hangout for Bridget and her friends in 'Bridget Jones's Diary', the novel by Helen Fielding that helped to make us a household name.

Show More

2002

Joining Tragus Group

Café Rouge is sold by Whitbread, becoming part of Tragus Group. Later on in 2015, Tragus became Casual Dining Group, consisting of Cafe Rouge, Bella Italia, Belgo, Las Iguanas, La Tasca & more.

Show More

2012

Award Winner

Café Rouge wins the Mediterranean Casual Dining category at London's prestigious MIDAS Awards.

Show More

2015

A New Look

Over two thirds of Café Rouge bistros were given a refurbishment in 2015, with classic and authentic interiors to complement our delicious food and wine. Our London flagship site opposite St. Paul’s Cathedral was given new life with a £500,000 refurbishment featuring a beautiful mural, specially commissioned for the 182-cover restaurant inside the magnificent Condor House.

2017

Sharing Our Expertise

We started partnering with Ducasse Education to offer our Chefs the best in award-winning development and education. With our iconic brand and 30 year heritage, it's no surprise that when it comes to career opportunities and developing our teams – we know what we're doing!

2018

A Busy Year For Café Rouge

We were privileged to have some of the seafood on our menu officially certified with the Marine Stewardship Council’s (MSC) Blue Fish label, demonstrating our ongoing commitment to sustainability and preserving the condition of the planet. Did you know? All of our mussels, hake and cod are fully traceable all the way back to MSC certified sustainable fisheries.
We also launched our twist on Afternoon Tea in 2018… Perfect for catch-ups with friends, birthday celebrations, or for those "just because" occasions! You'll enjoy 3 tasty tiers of treats including delectable cakes, chocolate mousse, fruit scones with clotted cream and strawberry jam, plus decadent slices of our famous Croque Monsieur. All for just £12.50 per person - ooh la la! We also introduced a Kids' version for mini guests too.
