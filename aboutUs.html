Cafe Rouge

We Love What We Do

"The emphasis is on freshly prepared food using a range of locally based suppliers and producers, no fancy or complicated masterpieces – just great food from great ingredients. Our lunch & dinner menus are based on satisfying regional Italian specialities and change weekly allowing us to make the most of seasonal ingredients when they’re at their best."
